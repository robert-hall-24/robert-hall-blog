What is EQ & IQ?


EQ


EQ stand for Emotional Quotient or Emotional Intelligence. This is in my opinion is one of the most important things to learn. A high level definition of EQ is the . It's the ability to understand, use, and manage your emotions to communicate effectively, empathize with others, and overcome challenges. This helps with relationships, decision making and identifying opportunities. One of the tools I use to help build my EQ is Mindfulness. The great thing with Mindfulness is it pretty easy to start and there are ton of free apps you can use to practice this. I use an app called: Mindfulness on my apple watch this set to remind around lunch each day to push to do this.


IQ


IQ stands for Intelligence Quotient. It is scored that measured on how good are you at solving problems and logically thinking. This one way to measure Intelligence and I just want point out that as being dyslexic I didn't really fit the binary of these tests sometimes. So it is okay to use this one measure to test your Intelligence. But here is the big peak of High IQ. Studies show that people with high IQ excel in problem solving. Problem solving is huge part of being developer so this defiantly an Advantage.


Listen and Loop and Wrap Up

Listen and Loop


This is really good exercise to learn how listen and how it is to be heard. This is a good skill to learn as in any conversation you have in life listening and trying to understand the information being received is a great way to
build deeper connections in conversations. Also it helps you digest what you are hearing and correcting if you maybe mis reading the comments being said. Specially when you are being given verbal instructions it is important that you understand what is being said. Overall practicing listening and looping back is super important.


Wrap Up


Conclusion I would say that EQ, IQ and Listen/Loop are skills and measures we should be practicing to striving towards. This helps us navigate tough decisions in life. Build better relationships and connections. Problem solve more effectively and be curious in discussions. The great thing is there is no right way or correct way to approach this. It is simply just starting.
